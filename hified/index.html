HiFied - Forgotten Wifi Password Viewer

Introducing HiFied, the ultimate tool developed by Logic Realm that simplifies your Wi-Fi experience. With HiFied, you can effortlessly retrieve and view all previously connected Wi-Fi SSIDs and passwords. Gone are the days of searching through your devices or struggling to remember the credentials for your favorite networks. HiFied empowers you with a convenient solution, allowing you to effortlessly access and manage your Wi-Fi connections with ease. Say goodbye to the hassle of forgotten passwords and welcome a new level of convenience with HiFied by Logic Realm.

Key Features


  • Seamless Wi-Fi Password Retrieval: HiFied streamlines the process of accessing your saved Wi-Fi credentials. Simply open the app, and it automatically retrieves and displays all previously connected SSIDs and their corresponding passwords, eliminating the need to dig through device settings or manually input passwords.


  • Secure Password Storage: HiFied prioritizes the security of your Wi-Fi passwords. It employs robust encryption techniques to securely store your credentials within the app, ensuring that your sensitive information remains protected and inaccessible to unauthorized users.


  • Intuitive User Interface: HiFied offers a user-friendly interface that makes navigating and managing your Wi-Fi connections effortless. The app organizes and presents the saved SSIDs and passwords in a clear and organized manner, allowing you to quickly find the network you need and retrieve its password with just a few taps. Its intuitive design ensures a seamless user experience for users of all technical backgrounds.

System Requirements


  • Operating System: Windows

  • Processor: 1.5 GHz or higher

  • Memory: 2 GB RAM

  • Storage: 30 MB available space
